# Adversarial Spec Review Prompt

Attack this spec as if you were trying to prove it is too ambitious or internally inconsistent.

Focus on:

- whether Wire-first is too large a scope for v1
- whether any part of the Codex quality bar should be deferred explicitly
- where the job model may be underspecified
- where review gate or session semantics are riskier than the documents admit

Return:

- the top architectural risks
- the top overspecified areas
- the top underspecified areas
- what should be cut or deferred if needed
